    Projects Take a look at some of the projects I have worked on!

    ROSO: Improving Robotic Policy Inference via Synthetic Observations

    Featuring my final year project at Monash University. Our research propose the use of generative AI to enhance zero-shot performance in robotic systems. Our approach, Robotic Policy Inference via Synthetic Observations (ROSO), employs stable diffusion to adapt observations of novel objects during inference, improving adaptability without extensive fine-tuning. Experiments demonstrate up to a 57% improvement in successful outcomes compared to pre-trained policies

    Paraview 3D particle Visualiser

    As a software engineer intern at DeepFlow, I had an amazing opportunity to develop open-source software that enables the creation of high-quality, detailed 3D visualizations of data using ParaView and Python. This advanced 3D particle visualisation software allows you to unlock the full potential of your data by creating high-quality, detailed 3D visualisations. Designed for material science, physics simulation etc. This powerful tool offers a range of features, including data table conversion, customizable colouring and lighting, thresholding, and animation.
